RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Library will be open from 8.15 am to 4.30 pm on all working days.
  • s student can borrow only one book at time
  • A book will be issued for a maximum period of 15 days and may be renewed only once.
  • Books may be renewed on the expiry of the due date, provided there is no reservtion for the same
  • Non-reeturn of books with in the stipulated time would entail a fine of Rs.2-/-per day for the frist five days & there after Rs.10/- per day will be levied.
  • The books in the reference section will not be issued. These will be available for reading in the library.
  • Access to the library and its services may be suspended to those violating the rules of the library.
  • Borrowed books are non-transferable and browser shall remain responsible for material issued for him/her until the material is returned.
  • Users make sure that books issued to him/her do not have any damage before leaving the issue counter. Any damage or loss of issued books will be compensated with appropriate fine.
  • Strict silence should be observed in and around the library.
